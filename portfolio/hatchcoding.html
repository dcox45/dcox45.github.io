Hatch Coding Canada Inc.

Hatch Coding Canada organizes coding classes throughout the Greater Toronto Area. This includes basic JavaScript projects for beginners, as well as more math-based python projects for more advanced coders.


My Work as JavaScript and Python Instructor:

At Hatch, my role was to direct classes of young learners (pre-K through 12th grade) in JavaScript using the Hatch company online platform. I was also tasked with teaching more advanced Python classes, requiring strong math and problem-solving skills.

This role also involved promoting the company across the Greater Toronto Area - travelling to public and private schools and encouraging students to sign up. Over the course of nearly a year, I have recruited dozens of students to the Hatch program, helping to generate thousands of dollars in revenue.
