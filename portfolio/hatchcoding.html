Hatch Coding Canada Inc.

Hatch Coding organizes coding classes throught the Greater Toronto Area. This includes basic JavaScript projects for beginners, as well as more math-based python projects for more advanced learners.

My Work as JavaScript and Python Instructor

At Hatch, my role was to direct classes of young learners (pre-K through 12th grade) in JavaScript using the Hatch company online platform. I was also tasked with teaching more advanced Python classes, which required strong math and problem-solving skills.

This role also involved promoting the company across the GTA - travelling to public and private schools and encouraging students to sign up. Over the course of nearly a year, I have recruited dozens of students to the Hatch program, helping to generate revenue.
