Toggl Augmented Reality

Toggl AR is a maker of Augmented Reality experiences. With toggl's app, users can spawn and interact with a variety of objects and games, and invite their friends to experience a shared virtual space.


My Work as a Unity 3D Developer

Augemented Reality experiences were developed for the Toggl app using the Unity 3D engine, with building and testing taking place via Xcode and iOS.

My role was to integrate 3D assets into the engine, giving them physics and behavior, with the aim of producing positive and fun experiences for users that can be shared with friends. Interactive objects include items such as a boombox, basketball net, and much more!

As a junior developer (working remotely from home), it was expected that I take the necessary steps to familiarize myself with Unity's asset pipeline and development conventions, as well as best practices for scripting behaviour with the C# language.

This led to an intense but fruitful learning process of developing and testing Unity scenes from start to finish, and reading up on coding fundamentals that I had never really encountered as someone coming from a non-computer science background.
