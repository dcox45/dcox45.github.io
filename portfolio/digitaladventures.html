Digital Education Adventures Inc.

Digital Adventures is a tech education startup company in Chicago, IL. The company instructs young learners in coding and emerging tech, sporting a uniquely diverse array of courses, from Ruby on Rails to Minecraft Modding.

My Work as Lead Technology Instructor

This story begins with sheer coincidence. While riding my bike one day, I passed by the Lincoln Park studio of DA. Looking inside, I noticed that their roof was leaking, and water was pooling on the floor of the room - which was full of expensive tech equipment. It was a Sunday, and so there was nobody in the studio.

So I grabbed on of DA's flyers on the front door, called up my future boss, Arjun, and informed him of the situation. After thanking me, I eventually asked Arjun if DA was hiring, and they were.

Over the course of about 2 months, I was given crash courses in coding fundamentals, while practicing specific projects on my own time. It was my role to then teach these projects to students.

By the summer of 2016, plans were made to have me oversee the studio I worked in, as the company expanded to other parts of Chicago. This new role would involve full responsibility over the Lincoln Park studio. Unfortunately, the work visa application did not work out, and I had to leave the United States back to Toronto. I wish Digital Adventures the best of luck!
