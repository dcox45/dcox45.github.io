Uchicago - Social Sciences Division

Known for its emphasis on interdisciplinary perspectives, the MAPSS (Master of Arts Program in the Social Sciences) program at the University of Chicago was one year of intense personal growth and academic study.

My work as a graduate student focused on:

  • The interplay between technology and environmental change
  • The Anthropocene thesis and its critiques
  • The rise of remote sensing technology
  • New media analysis - especially interactive documentary
  • UX (User Experience) research (into the above)
  • Geography of Canada and the United States
